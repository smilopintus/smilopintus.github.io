Dominion City - About us

Dominion City (New Covenant Family) is a ministry with a vision to preach the total gospel of Jesus Christ. We believe that when this is achieved, the lives of men will be transformed to reflect the image of Jesus Christ. This will eventually result in the raising of leaders that will transform the society.
